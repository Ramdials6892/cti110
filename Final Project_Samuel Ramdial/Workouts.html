Workouts

  • By making such an important lifestyle change, it's important to make it a habit. Remaining consistent, and maintaining the exercise routine, you'll be able to make it last long term.
  • Make a plan, and set realisitc goals. By setting short term goals, and long term goals, you'll continue to stay motivated and want to see more progress.
  • Remain Hydrated, and remember to stick to the workout routine. Some exercises may be difficult, but you can find modification that'll be easier for you to complete as you work your way up.
  • Whether it's your first time working out, or you're continuing from where you left off, it's extremely important to pay attention to your form. If you're not careful, you may just end up injuring yourself. You can find videos on youtube on how to properly perform these exercises.
